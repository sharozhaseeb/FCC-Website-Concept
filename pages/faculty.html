HEC POLICY AGAINST SEXUAL HARRASMENT

Effective immediately, FCCU will be implementing HEC Policy on Protection Against Sexual Harassment. We have implemented and been following the law. The HEC policy adds items that make it more helpful for students. The policy is posted on our website. The following employees are nominated to facilitate the implementation of the policy. They will hold this office for a period of two years.

Focal Persons:

Ms. Aisha Ateeq (University Psychology Department) Dr. Ivan Suneel (University Psychology Department/V.R. College) Academic Inquiry Committee (for hearing complaints against faculty & students)
