Latest News

The Baccalaureate and Postgraduate program at FCCU is an international standard, liberal arts program, offered at local prices, and taught by highly qualified faculty. It gives you the freedom to choose your own major, the subjects you will study each semester, and even your instructors. With a focus on research and development, the Postgraduate programs at FCCU are taught by faculty of international repute. State-of-the-art laboratories and research facilities, seminars, symposia and workshops supplement the academic programs.
