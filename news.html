Message From The Dean

Since the upgradation of FCC to a University, it is being developed on modern lines to give due importance to research while meeting all the requirements of liberal arts education. In order to meet the objectives of a University, the faculty at FCCU is actively engaged in a great variety of ongoing research activities and projects. Efforts have been made to encourage multi and interdisciplinary approach to research. These activities span the entire range of all of the disciplines that are represented at FCCU namely Humanities, Social and Natural Sciences, Business, Public Policy, and Education. We have initiated postgraduate programs in all these disciplines.

OBJECTIVE

“ To promote strategic partnership with industry in global knowledge economy to intensify the partnerships that go beyond the traditional funding of discrete research projects
To promote entrepreneurship, technology-transfer and commercialization activities that energize and support the local and national economy”

Latest News

The Baccalaureate and Postgraduate program at FCCU is an international standard, liberal arts program, offered at local prices, and taught by highly qualified faculty. It gives you the freedom to choose your own major, the subjects you will study each semester, and even your instructors. With a focus on research and development, the Postgraduate programs at FCCU are taught by faculty of international repute. State-of-the-art laboratories and research facilities, seminars, symposia and workshops supplement the academic programs.
